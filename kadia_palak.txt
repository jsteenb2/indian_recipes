INGREDIENTS
------------
200 gms paneer( Indian cottage cheese)
2 tbsp kadai masala powder
2 tablespoon oil/ghee
1 tsp ginger paste
1 tsp garlic paste
1 to 2 green chilies, chopped
2 medium sized onions, cut into 1 inch piece
5 medium tomatoes, chopped
1 inch piece of bell pepper(red, green, yellow)
1 teaspoon dry fenugreek leaves
Water as required
salt to taste
A few chopped coriander leaves for garnish(optional)

INSTRUCTIONS
------------
1) First prepare the kadai masala.
2) Chop the onions and capscium into 1 inch piece. Roughly chop tomatoes and keep the tomato aside.
3) In a wok heat oil or ghee, add the ginger-garlic paste and saute till their raw aroma disappears.
4) Then add the chopped onions and saute till transparent. Add green chillies.
5) Add 1 tbsp Kadai masala(save rest for using later for garnish) and saute for about 2-3 min.
6) Add the tomatoes and saute for 2 to 3 mins. Once the tomatoes become tender, add bell peppers, stir well and saute for few min.
7) After sauteing you will notice that oil is oozing out of the mixture. Add water, the quantity of water will depend on what consistency you want for gravy.
8) Sprinkle some salt and simmer for about 5-6 min on low heat. Open lid, you will see medium thick gravy has formed then, add paneer cubes and stir well.
9) Again cook for 3- 4 min with lid closed. Then add crushed dry fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)and sprinkle left (1 tbsp) kadai masala powder and stir.
10) Serve Kadai paneer hot along with naan, roti or pulao rice.
